# Vision

Build India's most configurable Training & Development Platform for SMEs,
Real Estate Companies and Enterprises.

The product must support:

- Cloud Deployment
- Self Hosted Deployment
- White Label Branding
- Multi Company
- Multi Branch
- Multi Department
- Multi Theme
- Mobile Responsive
- Production Ready

No vendor lock-in.

Backend should be replaceable without rewriting business logic.
